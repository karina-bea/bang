ABOUT

Bay Area Nerd Girls (BANG) is an interest-based meetup, members should be interested in any of the following: Sci-Fi, Anime/Manga, Comics, Cartoons, Academics, Design, RPG, Boardgames, Cosplay, and the list goes on. Whether you're a geek, nerd, or dork; it does not matter- every girl is welcome so long as she's open to meeting all types of friends.

BANG is open only to those who identify as a woman. We are emphatically trans- and queer-friendly. Must be 18+.
